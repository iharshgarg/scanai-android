Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2026

ScanAI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the ScanAI Android application.

Information We Collect

ScanAI does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

Camera Usage

ScanAI uses your device’s camera only when you choose to scan an image. The captured image is uploaded temporarily to our server for text recognition and sum calculation.

Data Processing

Images are processed solely to extract text and numbers. We do not store, share, or sell images or extracted data. Images are not retained after processing.

Permissions

ScanAI requires the following permissions:

  • Camera – to capture images for scanning
  • Internet – to send images to the server for processing

Children’s Privacy

ScanAI does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
